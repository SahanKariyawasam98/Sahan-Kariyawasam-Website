About Me

I am Sahan Chinthana Kariyawasam, a dedicated Chemical and Process Engineer who graduated from the University of Moratuwa in 2025. With extensive experience in developing industrial processes and designing innovative machinery, I am known for my strong problem-solving abilities and creative approach to engineering challenges.

My expertise spans across food and bioengineering, environmental engineering, and various industrial processes. I have a deep understanding of the principles and applications of these fields, which allows me to develop efficient and sustainable solutions. I am particularly passionate about leveraging my technical skills to optimize operations and contribute to sustainable solutions in the industry.

In the realm of food and bioengineering, I have worked on projects that involve the design and optimization of processes for the production of food and bio-based products. My work in this area has focused on improving product quality, enhancing process efficiency, and ensuring compliance with regulatory standards.

My interest in hygienic plant designing has led me to develop innovative solutions for maintaining cleanliness and preventing contamination in industrial settings. I have designed and implemented systems that ensure the highest standards of hygiene, which is crucial for industries such as food processing and pharmaceuticals.

Sustainability is a core value in my work. I am committed to developing processes and technologies that minimize environmental impact and promote the efficient use of resources. My work in sustainability concepts includes the development of waste reduction strategies, energy-efficient processes, and the use of renewable resources.

As a Lean Six Sigma Yellow Belt holder, I am committed to continuous improvement and efficiency in all my projects. I utilize Lean Six Sigma methodologies to identify and eliminate waste, improve process efficiency, and enhance product quality. This approach has enabled me to deliver high-quality results and drive innovation in the engineering field.

I look forward to applying my knowledge and skills to drive innovation and sustainability in the engineering field. I am always eager to take on new challenges and contribute to the success of my team and organization.
